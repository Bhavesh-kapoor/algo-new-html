Activate API Access

Subscribe to your broker's API service (Zerodha, Upstox, 5Paisa, etc.) through your broker account. API subscription fees vary by broker and are borne by the client.

Submit API Credentials

Receive a secure link from TheAlgoman to enter your API Key and Secret. Your credentials are encrypted and used solely for trade execution — we never store or misuse your login information.

3

Strategy Activation

Your chosen strategy is linked to your broker account, configured according to your capital, risk appetite, and preferred market segment (Equity, Options, or Futures).

Your algo goes live and executes trades automatically during market hours. Monitor all trades on your broker's app with full control to pause, stop, or exit trades anytime.
